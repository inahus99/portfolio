About Me

I’m a full-stack developer passionate about building real-time, scalable, and practical applications. My recent work focuses on privacy, monitoring, and legal-tech tools, where I combine strong engineering with modern stacks to solve real problems.

ComplyScan

A real-time compliance scanner that audits websites for GDPR/CCPA cookie violations and hidden trackers.

  • Leverages headless browser automation with Puppeteer for deep site analysis
  • Detects consent banners, 3P trackers, cookie lifetimes
  • Built with React, Node.js, Socket.io, and D3.js

Third-Eye

A website uptime & monitoring tool with instant health checks, analytics, and alerting.

  • Engineered for low-latency, distributed health checks via a robust job queue
  • Provides real-time status dashboards & API health endpoints
  • Stack: React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, deployed on Railway

An AI-powered legal-tech app that extracts, analyzes, and explains clauses from long PDF contracts.
